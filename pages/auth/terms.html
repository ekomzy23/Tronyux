Ronyx is an educational examination platform intended for students, lecturers, and institutions. You must:

  • Be at least 13 years of age (or have parental/guardian consent)

  • Have permission from your institution to use the platform for official examinations

You are responsible for keeping your login credentials secure. Do not share your password or OTP codes with anyone — including people claiming to be Ronyx staff (we will never ask for your password).

You are responsible for all activity that occurs under your account. Report any unauthorised access to your institution immediately.

4. Academic Integrity

Ronyx is built to support fair and honest assessment. By using the platform you agree that:

  • You will not share exam questions, access codes, or answers with others during or after an examination
  • You will not use unauthorised materials, tools, or assistance during a timed exam unless explicitly permitted
  • You will not attempt to manipulate scores, rankings, or attempt records
  • You will not use bots, scripts, or automated tools to interact with the platform

Violations may result in immediate account suspension and may be reported to your institution.

5. Acceptable Use

You agree not to:

  • Overload or interfere with the platform's servers or infrastructure
  • Use the platform for any purpose other than education and assessment

6. Privacy Policy — What We Collect

We collect the minimum information needed to run the platform:

  • Account data — your name, email address, and password (stored securely, never readable)
  • Profile data — your year/level and department (set by you, used to show relevant exams)
  • Exam data — your answers, submission times, scores, and attempt history
  • Notes and drafts — study notes and autosaved content you create
  • Uploaded files — documents you upload for upload-type exam questions

We do not collect your precise location, sell your data, or share it with advertisers.

7. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Authenticate you and keep your session secure

  • Calculate and display your scores and performance analytics

Your institution's administrators may view your exam scores and attempt history for academic reporting purposes.

8. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Appwrite Cloud, a managed backend platform with encryption at rest and in transit. Each user's data is isolated — you can only access your own records.

While we take security seriously, no system is 100% immune. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and enable all available security features.

To exercise any of these rights, contact your institution's Ronyx administrator or reach out through the Help section in the app.
